About Windfall

We're a four-piece progressive rock band from Belfast, Northern Ireland and we just recently released our first EP, Spiral. We're inspired by artists such This Town Needs Guns, LITE and Animals as Leaders, and although these influences shine through we aren't defined by them; we're our own weird thing! Our music is pretty instrumentally technical and rhythmically complex, but we aim to keep the vocals accessible and catchy so anyone can appreciate our stuff.
